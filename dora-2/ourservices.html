Understanding Mental Health

As you navigate through your academic journey, it's essential toprioritize

not just your academic success but also your mental well-being . Mental

health plays a crucial role in your overall quality of life, affecting

your ability to build relationships, manage stress and anxiety, Because

of that Better Mental health is here for you !

What is Mental health ?

Mental Health refers to your emotional and social well-being It encompasses

how individuals think and feel in their daily life as well as how you cope

with stress, depression ,anxiety and how to make choices .Taking care of

your mental health is just as important as taking care of your physical health.
